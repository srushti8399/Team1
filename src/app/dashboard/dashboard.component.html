Platform for the next billion students

This project is for the use of users(mainly students) to participate in structured interviews, explore company drives, and learn from other person interview experience to amplify their confidence and make oneself ready for interviews. Students will connect with the expertise for evaluating their performance, based on the feedback from interviewer, users can make their interview better and can stand in this zealous world. This project fascinates users to share their own experiences with others and can connect expert and companies to users. This project targets to give interview practice to users to boost their performance, providing information about recruitment drives to explore and provide interview experience of others which provide roadmap for best performance.
